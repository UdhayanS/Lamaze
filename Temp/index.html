About our classes

All the classes are taken by internationally certified LAMAZE childbirth educators. The classes include all the things to support and empower the pregnant families. It includes all the concepts on pregnancy nutrition, trimesters, labor pain management strategies, childbirth, breastfeeding and early parenting. Lamaze International encourages us to offer childbirth education in a variety of formats throughout the childbearing year that meet the specific needs of you and your family. This may include a series of classes with one group of students, several independent classes, an all-day class, or one-to-one teaching. All the Classes online at the moment.. This can be discussed over a phone call or text message.
