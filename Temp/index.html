About our classes

All the classes are taken by internationally certified LAMAZE childbirth educators. The classes include all the things to support and empower the pregnant families. It includes all the concepts on pregnancy nutrition, trimesters, labor pain management strategies, childbirth, breastfeeding and early parenting. Lamaze International encourages us to offer childbirth education in a variety of formats throughout the childbearing year that meet the specific needs of you and your family. This may include a series of classes with one group of students, several independent classes, an all-day class, or one-to-one teaching. All the Classes online at the moment.. This can be discussed over a phone call or text message.

Frequently Asked Questions

Lamaze classes are a childbirth preparation approach designed to help expectant mothers manage labor and delivery by teaching relaxation techniques, various movements, and massage.

Childbirth classes offer insights into the pregnancy journey and the labor process, which can reduce your anxiety and better prepare you for childbirth and caring for your baby once they're born.

Staying physically fit can boost your chances of having a natural birth. It's a good idea to exercise regularly, aiming for 30 minutes of active movement five to six days each week. Being flexible will also be beneficial when it's time to push during labor. Consider doing prenatal yoga for 30 minutes one or two times a week.
